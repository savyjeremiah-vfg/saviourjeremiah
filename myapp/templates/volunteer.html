Join the Team

Volunteer with us: RCF workers are members of the Redeemed Christian Fellowship who volunteer their time and efforts to serve God and the community. They are passionate about spreading the word of God and helping those in need.


Volunteer With Us Through the Following Ministries

Drama.

Express faith through performance! Act, script, or help with stage production to convey messages in a fun and meaningful way.

Bible Study

Facilitate spiritual growth by leading or assisting in Bible study sessions. Join to encourage learning and sharing of God’s Word.

Media/Publicity

Help the church reach more people through social media, announcements, photography, and promotions. Perfect for tech-savvy or creative minds.

Prayer Department

– Be part of the team that uplifts the church in intercession, ensuring that every event and ministry is covered in prayer. Join to deepen your spiritual life and help others spiritually.

Technical/Organising Department

– Help plan and coordinate church events smoothly. If you love structure, logistics, and making things happen behind the scenes, this is your place!

Choir

Use your voice to glorify God! Join the choir to sing, worship, and inspire others with music. No matter your skill level, your contribution matters.

Sanitation –

Keep our church environment clean and welcoming. If you care about hygiene, organization, and creating a pleasant atmosphere, this department needs you!

Decoration

Bring creativity and beauty to our gatherings. Decorate venues, events, and celebrations to make every moment memorable.

Evangelism

Spread the good news beyond the church walls. Join this team to engage, mentor, and bring others to Christ.

Follow-up

Support new members and visitors, ensuring they feel welcomed and connected. Make a difference in someone’s spiritual journey.

Welfare

Care for the needs of church members. Provide emotional, social, and material support to those in need.

Academic Departments

Help fellow students with studies, mentoring, and educational programs. Share knowledge and encourage academic growth.

Sport

Promote fitness, teamwork, and fellowship through sports activities. Join to encourage healthy living and fun competitions!
