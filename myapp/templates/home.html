Welcome to RCF YABATECH

The Redeemed Christian Fellowship (RCF) is a dynamic, faith-based student organization affiliated with the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG). It is established in various higher institutions across Nigeria and beyond, serving as a platform for students to grow spiritually, build meaningful relationships, and fulfill their God-given purpose during their academic journey. RCF is committed to fostering spiritual growth through regular Bible studies, worship sessions, prayer meetings, and evangelistic outreach programs. The fellowship emphasizes the importance of living a Christ-centered life and equips students with the tools to navigate the challenges of campus life while staying rooted in their faith. RCF also creates opportunities for leadership development, encouraging members to take active roles in ministry and service, both within the fellowship and the broader community. With its welcoming atmosphere, RCF is a home away from home, where students can find support, encouragement, and a sense of belonging. Above all, RCF is driven by the mission to win souls for Christ and prepare believers for eternity, ensuring that students leave not only academically equipped but spiritually empowered to impact the world for God’s glory.
